# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Contributors are expected to communicate respectfully, assume good intent, and focus discussion on the work.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, abuse, discriminatory language, deliberate intimidation, and sharing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior does not meet this standard.

Security issues should be reported privately through GitHub private vulnerability reporting, not through public conduct channels.
